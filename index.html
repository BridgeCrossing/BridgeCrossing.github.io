Bridge Crossing


Hi! I'm Eric, a Bay Area native hailing from Palo Alto, currently residing in the Windy City of Chicago. I have always believed that there’s a special feeling one can experience while crossing a nice bridge. Often in Korean dramas and Japanese animation, a bridge is used as the setting for a budding romance or a lone-wolf protagonist's personal growth. I tend to relate more to the latter :)

Like countless millennials, I was drawn like a moth to the alluring flame of data science. I learned about the field after graduating with a background in environmental science. It took major career hacking to cross over into this world: years of health research at the CDC, Northwestern’s Data Science Boot Camp, and portfolio-building. In the end, I managed to bridge my academic background to the business world. The more I dive into my hard-earned role, the more I appreciate how versatile coding can be, from performing analytics to producing beautiful visuals.

During Chicago’s polar vortex and the 2020 pandemic, I accumulated an abundant amount of wanderlust. It is my hope to expand the artistic horizons of my skill set, and bridge my newly-earned coding background with romanticized storytelling of travel. I'm happy to have you along for the ride!
